Buzkashi

What it Buzkashi?
Buzkashi or "Goat Pulling" is a traditional Afghan game where there are two teams on horseback each fighting to throw a dead goat into a raised hole. The team with the most points wins. You may ask, "why a dead goat? It's 2020." To that I say, "why not?".
Origins
The game was created when waring tribes would steal eachother's goats on horseback, then they would chase the goat stealer. After they'd done it enough times it became fun, so they made a game out of it.
